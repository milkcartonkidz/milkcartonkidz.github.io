Wind chimes are easier to make than you might think. I've included a parts list below:

  • 3/4" steel EMT conduit
  • pipe cutter
  • drill with a 1/8" drill bit
  • measuring tape
  • heat shrink tubing
  • twine, or some kind of fishing line/rope/string
  • eyelet screws
  • two pieces of wooden discs, one smaller than the other
  • material for the wind sail.. could be plastic, wood, glass, etc.
  • weatherproof wood stain
